***Arch Setup***
****Alacritty**********


Alacritty is a gpu accelerated terminal for running bash

Arch Wiki: https://wiki.archlinux.org/title/Alacritty

Github: https://github.com/alacritty/alacritty
Themes_Github: https://github.com/toggle-corp/alacritty-colorscheme/blob/master/README.md

Changing themes: alacritty-colorscheme -V apply <theme-name>.yaml
                    *this works with themes in ~/.config/alacritty/colors

Toggles: See Themes_Github for advice on toggling between themes
